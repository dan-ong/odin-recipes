Japanese Chicken Curry

Description

Serves 6.
Japanese Chicken Curry (aka Kare-raisu) prepared in a pressure cooker!

Ingredients

  • 3 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 680g chicken thigh
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp neutral-flavoured cooking oil
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 package Japanese curry roux (200-230g)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Method

  1. Cut onions in half and then cut each half into 4-5 wedges.
  2. Peel carrots and cut using 'rangiri' technique.
  3. Peel potatoes and cut in half or quarters, depending on size.
  4. Mince ginger and garlic
  5. Cut meat into bite sized pieces (sogigiri cutting technique). Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Hit the 'Saute' button on the instant pot and add oil when pot is hot.
  7. Add onions, garlic and ginger, cook until onions are slightly translucent.
  8. Add chicken to the pot and mix.
  9. Add carrots and potatoes to the pot and mix well.
  10. Add the chicken stock and press ingredients down.
  11. Add curry roux blocks on top of the other ingredients and do not mix - leave the roux on top.
  12. Cover and lock lid. Use the Meat/Stew setting for 15 mins.
  13. When done, allow the pot to pressure release naturally for 10 mins.
  14. Add tomato paste and soy sauce. Mix well. Serve with rice!
